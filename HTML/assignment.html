Portfolio Site Map

In this stage, you have to create a sitemap to archive your goals which you gather on stage one. The sitemap provides the foundation for any well-designed website. It helps give web designers a clear idea of the website’s information architecture and explains the relationships between the various pages and content elements. Building a site without a sitemap is like building a house without a blueprint. And that rarely turns out well.
